## 📌 Featured Projects

### 🏡 [Real Estate Listing Intelligence System](https://github.com/qiwen-wen/real-estate-nlp)
End-to-end NLP pipeline for MLS listings built during a 12-week internship at IDX Exchange. Features semantic search (FAISS + sentence-transformers), named entity extraction, natural language → SQL query parsing with injection protection, and buyer intent classification.
`Python` `FAISS` `HuggingFace` `scikit-learn` `Docker` `MySQL`

### 📈 [WallStreetBets Stock Heat Predictor](https://github.com/qiwen-wen/wallstreetbets-sentiment-analysis) · [Live Demo](https://qiwen-wen.github.io/wallstreetbets-sentiment-analysis/)
Predicts next-day stock volatility from Reddit r/WallStreetBets sentiment. Final model achieves **AUC 0.80** on 16,645 ticker-days across 78 stocks, combining TF-IDF text features, VADER sentiment, and lagged price/volume signals.
`Python` `scikit-learn` `NLTK` `yfinance` `Plotly`

### 🇧🇷 [Brazil Labor Market Research](https://github.com/qiwen-wen/brazil-labor-market-research)
Scraped and analyzed 10,000+ job postings from Brazil's largest job platform to study regional salary disparities. Conducted under PhD supervision at the UCSD Economics Lab. Found job complexity (r=0.39) and cost of living (r=0.25) are stronger salary predictors than regional GDP.
`Python` `BeautifulSoup` `pandas` `Matplotlib`

### 📊 [UCSD STEM Course Insights](https://github.com/qiwen-wen/UCSD-Course-Insights)
Analyzed 61,791 CAPE student evaluations to identify what drives course satisfaction across 14 STEM departments. Instructor quality (Pearson r=0.79) emerged as the dominant factor over workload, class size, and grade expectations.
`Python` `pandas` `scipy` `statsmodels` `seaborn`
